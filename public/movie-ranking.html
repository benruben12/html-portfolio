The Best Movies According to Ruben

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Squid Game: The Challenge

I love the Squid Game movie because it blends intense storytelling, thought-provoking themes, and gripping suspense into a uniquely captivating and unforgettable experience.

Spiderman

I love the Spider-Man movie because it combines thrilling action, relatable characters, and a compelling narrative that keeps me on the edge of my seat.

Rambo

I love the Rambo movie because it showcases a relentless and iconic protagonist fighting for justice, showcasing intense action sequences, and exploring themes of resilience and heroism.
